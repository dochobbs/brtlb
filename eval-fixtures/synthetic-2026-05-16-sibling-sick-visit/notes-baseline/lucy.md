**Chief Complaint**
"She woke up cranky... pulling at her right ear."

**HPI**
Lucy is an 18-month-old female presenting with acute onset of right ear pulling and rhinorrhea, which began this morning upon waking. Mother reports she has been cranky. She nursed well this morning but refused solids (oatmeal). There has been no fever reported at home.

**ROS**
- Constitutional: No fever. Reports crankiness.
- HEENT: Reports right ear pulling and rhinorrhea.
- GI: Decreased appetite for solids, tolerating nursing well.
- Respiratory: No work of breathing observed.

**Exam**
- General: In no acute distress.
- HEENT:
    - Ears: **Right tympanic membrane is erythematous, dull, and slightly bulging with loss of landmarks.** Left TM is normal.
    - Nose: Clear rhinorrhea present.
    - Oropharynx: Mild postnasal drip noted. Mucosa is clear.
- Lungs: Clear to auscultation bilaterally.

**Assessment**
1.  Acute Otitis Media, right ear
2.  Viral Upper Respiratory Infection

**Plan**
1.  **Right Acute Otitis Media:**
    - Due to age (>6 mo), recent onset, and absence of fever or severe symptoms, will proceed with watchful waiting.
    - Provided a safety-net prescription for Amoxicillin. Mother instructed to fill only if Lucy is not improving in 48 hours, or if symptoms worsen sooner.
    - For pain, recommended ibuprofen 6 mL of children's formulation every 6 hours as needed. Advised not to exceed 4 doses in 24 hours.

2.  **Health Maintenance:**
    - Immunizations (Hepatitis A #1, Varicella #2) are due but were deferred today due to acute illness.

3.  **General Care & Precautions:**
    - Patient may return to daycare when comfortable, as otitis media is not contagious.
    - Counseled on general household illness prevention (hand washing, not sharing cups).
    - Return precautions: Return to clinic sooner if she develops a high fever, if symptoms worsen, or if she is not improving by day 5-6 of illness.

**Follow-up**
- Return to clinic in 3-4 weeks for deferred immunizations.
- Return sooner as needed per precautions above.
